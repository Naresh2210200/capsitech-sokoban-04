This Privacy Policy describes how Box Push ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

Box Push does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require an account, login, or internet connection to play.

The App saves your game progress (current level, move counts, best scores) locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device, is not transmitted to us or any third party, and is deleted if you uninstall the App.

Advertising and Third-Party Services

Box Push does not currently display ads or use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. If this changes in a future update, this policy will be updated in advance to describe what is collected and by whom.

Permissions

Box Push does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, location, storage, or any other sensitive device permissions.

Children's Privacy

Box Push does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. Since no data is collected at all, no personal information is gathered from users of any age.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any information with third parties, because no information is collected in the first place.

Changes to This Policy

If we add features that involve data collection in the future (such as ads, analytics, or cloud saves), this Privacy Policy will be updated first, with a new "Last updated" date, before that feature is released.
